What is RAG?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation is a technique that combines the power of large language models (LLMs) with the ability to retrieve specific information from your databases, documents and internal systems.

Instead of having a generalist AI that sometimes "hallucinates", you get an assistant that :

  • Accesses YOUR data in real time
  • Cites its sources in a verifiable way
  • Stays up to date with your latest information
  • Minimizes errors and hallucinations

How it works ?

1

Indexing

Your documents are transformed into searchable vector embeddings

2

Search

For each question, the system finds the most relevant passages

3

Generation

The AI generates an answer based on these passages, with citations
